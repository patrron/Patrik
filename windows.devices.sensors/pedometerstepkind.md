# PedometerStepKind

## -description
The type of step taken according to the pedometer.

## -enum-fields
### -field Unknown:0
An unknown step type.

### -field Walking:1
A walking step.

### -field Running:2
A running step.


## -remarks
A single step type applies to all of the steps recorded during a single [PedometerReading](pedometerreading.md).
