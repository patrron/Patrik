# Windows.Graphics.Display.DisplayProperties.StereoEnabled

## -description
> [!NOTE]
> [DisplayProperties](displayproperties.md) may be altered or unavailable for releases after WindowsÂ 8.1. Instead, use [DisplayInformation](displayinformation.md).

 Gets a value that indicates whether the device supports stereoscopic 3D.

## -property-value
**TRUE** if the device is capable of stereoscopic 3D and stereoscopic 3D is currently enabled; **FALSE** otherwise.
