# Windows.Storage.FileProperties.StorageItemThumbnail.CloneStream

## -description
Creates a new stream over the thumbnail that is represented by the current [storageItemThumbnail](storageitemthumbnail.md) object.

## -returns
The new thumbnail stream. The initial, internal position of the stream is 0.

The internal position and lifetime of this new thumbnail stream are independent from the position and lifetime of the cloned stream.
