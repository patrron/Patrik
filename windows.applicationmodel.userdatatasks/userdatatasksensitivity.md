# Windows.ApplicationModel.UserDataTasks.UserDataTaskSensitivity

## -description
Specifies the sensitivity of a task.

## -enum-fields

## -field Public:0
The task is publicly available to view.

## -field Private:1
The task is private and can't be viewed publicly.
