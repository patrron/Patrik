# EmailMeetingResponseType

## -description
Defines the type of response to a meeting request.

## -enum-fields
### -field Accept:0
The meeting was accepted.

### -field Decline:1
The meeting was declined.

### -field Tentative:2
The meeting was tentatively accepted.
