# ContactCardTabKind

## -description
Specifies which tab to display on a mini contact card.

## -enum-fields
### -field Default:0
The default tab.

### -field Email:1
The email tab.

### -field Messaging:2
The messaging tab.

### -field Phone:3
The phone tab.

### -field Video:4
The video tab.

### -field OrganizationalHierarchy:5
The organizational hierarchy tab.
