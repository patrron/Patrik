# Windows.Media.Editing.MediaOverlay.Opacity

## -description
Gets the opacity of the overlay.

## -property-value
The opacity of the overlay, in the range of 0.0 to 1.0, where 0.0 is completely transparent and 1.0 is completely opaque.
