# Windows.Media.Editing.MediaClip.OriginalDuration

## -description
Original playback time of the media clip, without the effects of [TrimTimeFromStart](mediaclip_trimtimefromstart.md) and [TrimTimeFromEnd](mediaclip_trimtimefromend.md) properties.

## -property-value
The original duration of the media clip is equal to [TrimmedDuration](mediaclip_trimmedduration.md) + [TrimTimeFromStart](mediaclip_trimtimefromstart.md) + [TrimTimeFromEnd](mediaclip_trimtimefromend.md).
