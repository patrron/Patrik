# Windows.Media.Protection.PlayReady.INDDownloadEngine.Notifier

## -description
Gets the download engine notifier that will provide notification of download stream events from the transmitter.

## -property-value
A download engine notifier created from the [INDDownloadEngineNotifier](inddownloadenginenotifier.md) interface.
