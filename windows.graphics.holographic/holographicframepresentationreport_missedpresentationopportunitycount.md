# Windows.Graphics.Holographic.HolographicFramePresentationReport.MissedPresentationOpportunityCount

## -description
The number of presentation opportunities which were missed before the app's frame was finished rendering.

## -property-value

## -remarks
Normally zero; may be negative if the app requested a head start and finished a full frame early.
