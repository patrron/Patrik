# Windows.UI.Xaml.Controls.Maps.MapControl.Pitch

## -description
Gets the actual pitch of the map. Sometimes the pitch requested by setting the [DesiredPitch](mapcontrol_desiredpitch.md) property cannot be set.

## -property-value
The actual pitch of the map. Sometimes the pitch requested by setting the property cannot be set.
