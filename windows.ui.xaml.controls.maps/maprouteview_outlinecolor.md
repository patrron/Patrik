# Windows.UI.Xaml.Controls.Maps.MapRouteView.OutlineColor

## -description
Gets or sets the color of the outline of the route displayed in the [MapRouteView](maprouteview.md).

## -property-value
The color of the outline of the route displayed in the [MapRouteView](maprouteview.md).

## -remarks
By default, the fill color of the route does not have a colored outline.
