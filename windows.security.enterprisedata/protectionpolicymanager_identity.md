# Windows.Security.EnterpriseData.ProtectionPolicyManager.Identity

## -description
> [!NOTE]
> Windows Information Protection (WIP) policy cannot be applied on WindowsÂ 10, version 1511 (build 10586) or earlier.

Gets or sets the enterprise identity.

## -property-value
The enterprise identity. This is an email address or domain that is managed. Your app should use [IsIdentityManaged](protectionpolicymanager_isidentitymanaged_906801364.md) to confirm that an email address or domain is managed.
