# Windows.UI.Input.Inking.InkPoint.Position

## -description
The X, Y coordinates of the [InkPoint](inkpoint.md), in device-independent pixel (DIP) relative to the upper left-hand corner of the inking area.

## -property-value
The X, Y coordinates relative to the upper left-hand corner of the inking area.
