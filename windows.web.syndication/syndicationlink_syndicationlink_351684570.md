# Windows.Web.Syndication.SyndicationLink.SyndicationLink

## -description
Creates a new [SyndicationLink](syndicationlink.md) object with a **Uri**, **Relationship**, **Title**, **MediaType**, and **Length**.

## -parameters
### -param uri
The [Uri](../windows.foundation/uri.md) value.

### -param relationship
The relationship type.

### -param title
The title of the syndication link.

### -param mediaType
The MediaType of the syndication link.

### -param length
The length, in bytes, of the syndication link.
