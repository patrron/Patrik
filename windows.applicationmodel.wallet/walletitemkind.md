# WalletItemKind

## -description
Represents the kinds (classifications) of wallet items supported.

## -enum-fields
### -field Invalid:0
The item is invalid.

### -field Deal:1
A deal.

### -field General:2
A general item.

### -field PaymentInstrument:3
A payment instrument.

### -field Ticket:4
A ticket.

### -field BoardingPass:5
A boarding pass.

### -field MembershipCard:6
A membership card.
