# Windows.Media.Protection.HdcpSession.IsEffectiveProtectionAtLeast

## -description
Checks whether the effective protection of the [HdcpSession](hdcpsession.md) instance is at least equal to the given [HdcpProtection](hdcpprotection.md) value.

## -parameters
### -param protection
The level of protection to check against the [HdcpSession](hdcpsession.md) instance's protection level.

## -returns
True if the protection level of the [HdcpSession](hdcpsession.md) is at least equal to the value of *protection*.
