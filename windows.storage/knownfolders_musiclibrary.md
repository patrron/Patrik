# Windows.Storage.KnownFolders.MusicLibrary

## -description
Gets the Music library.

## -property-value
The Music library.

## -remarks
To access the Music library, in the app manifest, specify the **Music Library** capability. For info about accessing the Music, Pictures, or Videos library in a Universal Windows app, see [Files and folders in the Music, Pictures, and Videos libraries](https://msdn.microsoft.com/library/1ae29512-7a7d-4179-adac-f02819ac2c39).

> The Music Library typically has the following path.

+ `%USERPROFILE%\Music`
