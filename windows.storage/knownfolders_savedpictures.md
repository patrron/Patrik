# Windows.Storage.KnownFolders.SavedPictures

## -description
Gets the Saved Pictures folder.

## -property-value
The Saved Pictures folder.

## -remarks
To access the Saved Pictures folder, in the app manifest, specify the **Pictures Library** capability.
