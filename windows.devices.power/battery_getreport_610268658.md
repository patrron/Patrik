# Windows.Devices.Power.Battery.GetReport

## -description
Gets a [BatteryReport](batteryreport.md) object that indicates the charge, capacity, and status of the battery or aggregate.

## -returns
Indicates the charge, capacity, and status of the battery or aggregate.
