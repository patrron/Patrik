# Windows.Networking.Sockets.SocketActivityInformation.StreamSocket

## -description
Based on the socket type, the app can reclaim the ownership of the appropriate [StreamSocket](streamsocket.md).

## -property-value
The [StreamSocket](streamsocket.md) to reclaim.
