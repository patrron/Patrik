# Windows.Networking.Sockets.SocketActivityInformation.StreamSocketListener

## -description
Based on the socket type, the app can reclaim the ownership of the appropriate [StreamSocketListener](streamsocketlistener.md).

## -property-value
The [StreamSocketListener](streamsocketlistener.md) to reclaim.
