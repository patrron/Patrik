# Windows.Networking.Sockets.SocketActivityInformation.DatagramSocket

## -description
Based on the socket type, the app can reclaim the ownership of the appropriate [DatagramSocket](datagramsocket.md).

## -property-value
The [DatagramSocket](datagramsocket.md) to reclaim.
