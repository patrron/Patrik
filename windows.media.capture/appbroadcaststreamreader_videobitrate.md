# Windows.Media.Capture.AppBroadcastStreamReader.VideoBitrate

## -description
Gets the bitrate of the video stream associated with the [AppBroadcastStreamReader](appbroadcaststreamreader.md), in bits per second.

## -property-value
The bitrate of the video stream, in bits per second.
