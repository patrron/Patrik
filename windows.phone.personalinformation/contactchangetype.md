# ContactChangeType

## -description
Indicates the type of change represented by a [ContactChangeRecord](contactchangerecord.md).

## -enum-fields
### -field Created:0
The contact was created.

### -field Modified:1
The contact was modified.

### -field Deleted:2
The contact was deleted.
