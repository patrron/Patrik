# Windows.Devices.PointOfService.JournalPrinterCapabilities.IsReversePaperFeedByLineSupported

## -description
Gets whether the journal printer station can reverse paper feed by line.

## -property-value
True if the journal printer station can reverse paper feed by line; otherwise false.
