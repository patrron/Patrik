# Windows.Devices.PointOfService.ReceiptPrintJob.FeedPaperByMapModeUnit

## -description
Adds an instruction to the print job to feed paper by map mode unit on the receipt printer station.

## -parameters
### -param distance
The number of map mode units to feed on the receipt printer. Use a positive number to feed paper forward or a negative number to feed paper backward.
