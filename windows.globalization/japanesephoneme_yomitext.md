# Windows.Globalization.JapanesePhoneme.YomiText

## -description
Gets the "reading" (the pronunciation of [DisplayText](japanesephoneme_displaytext.md)) for this Japanese word.

## -property-value
The "reading" (the pronunciation of [DisplayText](japanesephoneme_displaytext.md), represented by a sequence of Hiragana characters).
