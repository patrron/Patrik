# Windows.Networking.Vpn.VpnTrafficFilterAssignment.AllowOutbound

## -description
Gets or sets whether outbound traffic is allowed or not.

## -property-value
If outbound traffic is allowed, it is **TRUE**; otherwise , it is **FALSE**.
