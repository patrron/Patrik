# Windows.Networking.Vpn.VpnTrafficFilterAssignment.AllowInbound

## -description
Gets or sets whether inbound traffic is allowed or not.

## -property-value
If inbound traffic is allowed, it is **TRUE**; otherwise , it is **FALSE**.
