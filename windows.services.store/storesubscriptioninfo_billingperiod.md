# Windows.Services.Store.StoreSubscriptionInfo.BillingPeriod

## -description
Gets the duration of the billing period for a subscription, in the units specified by the [BillingPeriodUnit](storesubscriptioninfo_billingperiodunit.md) property.

## -property-value
The duration of the billing period, in the units specified by the [BillingPeriodUnit](storesubscriptioninfo_billingperiodunit.md) property.
