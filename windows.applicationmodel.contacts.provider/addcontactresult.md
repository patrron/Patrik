# AddContactResult

## -description
Indicates whether a contact was added successfully.

## -enum-fields
### -field Added:0
The contact was added.

### -field AlreadyAdded:1
The contact was already selected by the user.

### -field Unavailable:2
The contact was unavailable and was not added.
